1. Hold the multimeter in your hand.
2. Take the red cable and connect it to the right most port of the multimeter
3. Connect the black cable to the second port from the right of the multimeter.
4. Turn the multimeter on by pushing the green power button. You should see the display turn on.
5. Turn the dial on the multimeter to the ohm symbol.
6. Take the resistor and connect one side of it to the red cable and the other side to the black cable.
7. The number on the display shows the resistance of the resistor.
