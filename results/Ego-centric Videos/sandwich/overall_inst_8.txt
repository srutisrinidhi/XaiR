1. Place two slices of gluten-free bread on a wooden surface.
2. Spread butter on both slices of bread.
3. Open a jar of Mulberry, Raspberry & Blueberry Jam and place it next to the bread slices.
4. Spread jam on one slice of bread.
5. Spread cream cheese on the other slice of bread.
6. Press the two slices together to make a sandwich.
7. Cut the sandwich into smaller pieces using a knife on a wooden cutting board.
8. Arrange the sandwich pieces on a plate for serving.
