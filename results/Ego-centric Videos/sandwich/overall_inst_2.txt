Instructions:
1. Open a package of sliced bread and place two slices on a wooden surface.
2. Spread Nuttelex Original or a similar spread on the two slices of bread using a knife.
3. Evenly spread butter on one slice of multigrain bread using a knife.
4. Spread jam on the other slice of bread that already has a spread on it.
5. Place the two slices of bread with different spreads together to make a sandwich.
6. Cut the sandwich into quarters to serve on a plate.
7. Arrange the quartered sandwich pieces neatly on the plate for serving.
