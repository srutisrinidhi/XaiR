1. Unwrap and open sandwich package. 
2. Place two slices of bread on a wooden cutting board. 
3. Spread butter or margarine on each slice of bread using a knife. 
4. Place a jar of mixed berry jam on the cutting board. 
5. Spread jam on one slice of bread. 
6. Place the other slice with a spreadable cheese and then jam on top to make a sandwich. 
7. Cut the sandwich into smaller pieces. 
8. Arrange the sandwich pieces on a plate in a neat pattern.
