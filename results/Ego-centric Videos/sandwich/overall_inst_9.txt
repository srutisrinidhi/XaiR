1. Place two slices of gluten-free bread on a wooden surface.
2. Spread Nuttelex Original plant-based butter alternative on the slices of bread using a knife.
3. Ensure the butter is spread evenly on both slices of bread.
4. Spread homemade mixed berry jam on one of the buttered bread slices using a knife.
5. Press the two slices of bread together to make a sandwich.
6. Cut the sandwich into four triangles using a knife.
7. Arrange the sandwich triangles on a plate for serving.
