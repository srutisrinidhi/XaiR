Instructions:
1. Place two slices of bread on a wooden surface.
2. Spread Nuttelex Original or butter evenly on each slice.
3. Present and then spread homemade jam on top of the buttered bread slices.
4. Place one slice of bread with butter and the other with jam together to make a sandwich.
5. Cut the sandwich into smaller pieces, such as triangles or quarters.
6. Rearrange the sandwich pieces on a plate to form a visually appealing display, alternating the direction of the pointed ends.
