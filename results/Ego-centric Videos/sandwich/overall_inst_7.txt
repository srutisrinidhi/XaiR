1. Open the package of gluten-free bread and take out two slices.
2. Butter both slices of bread with Nuttelex Original dairy-free spread.
3. Spread the butter evenly across each slice of bread using a knife.
4. Spread Multuerries/Raspberry & Blueberry Jam on one slice of bread.
5. Make a sandwich by putting the buttered slice and the jam-covered slice together.
6. Cut the sandwich in half.
7. Cut the sandwich halves into smaller triangles and arrange them on a plate.
8. Rearrange the triangular sandwich pieces on the plate to create a visually pleasing pattern like a fish, using a breadstick as the tail.
