1. Turn on and adjust the temperature of the soldering station.
2. Wait for the soldering iron to get to the desired temperature.
3. Break two header pins from a group of pins and insert them into a PCB board.
4. Unwrap some of the solder wire.
5. Wipe the soldering iron on a sponge pads
6. Apply solder on the PCB by touching the soldering iron and solder wire together.
7. Wipe the soldering iron on the sponge pad again before placing it back in its place.
7. Then take two more pins and attach it to the other end of the PCB.
8. Solder these componenets as well.
9. Inspect the pins that have been soldered into the PCB.
10. Connect two wires between the pins.
