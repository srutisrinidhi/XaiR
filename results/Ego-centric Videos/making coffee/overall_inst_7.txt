1. Open the coffee grinder lid and add coffee beans.
2. Grind the coffee beans and load the ground coffee into a portafilter.
3. Distribute and tamp the coffee grounds in the portafilter.
4. Insert the portafilter into the espresso machine.
5. Attach the portafilter to the espresso machine securely.
6. Start the espresso brewing process.
7. Pull the espresso shot into a cup.
8. Stop the espresso flow with a button or lever.
9. Clean the portafilter and knock out used coffee grounds.
10. Rinse the portafilter under a tap.
11. Clean the portafilter with paper towels.
12. Repeat the process for subsequent espresso shots.
13. Enjoy the prepared espresso and clean up the area afterward.
