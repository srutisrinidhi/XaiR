1. Examine and adjust the coffee grinder settings.
2. Grind coffee beans and fill the espresso machine portafilter.
3. Dose and tamper the coffee grounds in the portafilter.
4. Lock the portafilter into the espresso machine.
5. Insert the portafilter into the machine's group head.
6. Place a paper cup into the espresso machine and start the brewing process.
7. Monitor the shot extraction on the digital display.
8. Continue brewing the espresso shot until the cup is filled.
9. Increase pressure using the machine's lever during extraction.
10. Stop the shot, knock out used coffee grounds into a knock box, rinse the portafilter.
11. Clean the portafilter with a paper towel and water.
12. Discard used paper towel and cup in a trash bin.
