1. Check and fill the espresso grinder with coffee beans.
2. Secure the portafilter in the coffee grinder and grind the beans directly into it.
3. Adjust the settings on the grinder, tamp the coffee grounds in the portafilter, and attach it to the espresso machine.
4. Lock in the portafilter with the ground coffee into the espresso machine's group head.
5. Pull an espresso shot into a takeaway cup.
6. Discard the used coffee grounds from the portafilter and clean it under a sink.
7. Refill the portafilter with fresh grounds and attach it to the espresso machine to prepare a new shot of espresso.
