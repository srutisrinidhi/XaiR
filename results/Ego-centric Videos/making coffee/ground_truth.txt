1. Make sure the espresso grinder has coffee beans.
2. Insert the portafilter into the grinder.
3. grind the coffee beans and collect the grounds in the portafilter
4. Tamp the grounds in the portafilter
5. Insert the  portafilter into the espresso machine and lock it into place.
6. Place a cup under the portafilter to collect the coffee.
7. Use the lever to turn on the espresso machine and let the coffee drop into the cup.
8. Once done, turn off the machine and remove the portafilter.
9. Dump the used coffee grounds into the knock box or another bin.
10. Rinse the portafilter in water and dry it with a paper towl.
